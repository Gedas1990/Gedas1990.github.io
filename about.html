Biography

Kickboxing Club was set up in early 2020 and has grown from strength to strength to become well know and respected training facility. We specialise in Kickboxing with classes 7 days a week at our fully equipped trainign facilities located right in the heart of Dublin. All of the Kickboxing CLub's Black Belt instructors are highly motivated, extremely dedicated and very approachable. We love new members. At Kickboxing CLub we pride ourselves on our friendly enviroment and with new members starting all the time you will never find yourself out of place in our club. We have classes for both kids and adults and have both private and group classes available 7 days a week. No matter your age or fitness level we will get you fit fast. If you have any questions or queries please don't hesitate to contact us at questions@kickboxingclub.com or phone us at 01-822-7270
